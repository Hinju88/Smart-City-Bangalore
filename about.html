About Bangalore Smart City

Bangalore Smart City is a vision to make the city more efficient, sustainable, and livable by leveraging technology and innovation. From energy efficiency to citizen involvement, every aspect of the city is geared toward enhancing the quality of life and providing cutting-edge solutions.

Join us in transforming Bangalore into a city of the future. Together, we can create a smarter and better-connected world.

Bangalore, known as the Silicon Valley of India, is embracing the Smart City Mission to transform into a sustainable, technologically advanced urban hub. The Bangalore Smart City initiative focuses on leveraging cutting-edge technologies to improve infrastructure, public services, and quality of life for its citizens and visitors.

Key objectives include:

1.Efficient Public Transport: Integration of smart mobility solutions like app-based ticketing, real-time tracking of buses, and metro connectivity.
2.Energy Management: Promotion of renewable energy, energy-efficient systems, and smart grids.
3.Waste Management: Implementation of IoT-based solutions for waste collection and recycling.
4.Pollution Control: Real-time air quality monitoring and green initiatives to reduce carbon footprints.
5.Citizen Engagement: Platforms for transparent governance, grievance redressal, and participatory planning.
