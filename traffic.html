Smart Traffic Control

About Traffic Control

Smart Traffic Control utilizes IoT sensors, real-time monitoring, and AI-based analytics to streamline vehicle movement, reduce congestion, and improve road safety. By implementing intelligent traffic signals, we aim to optimize urban mobility while lowering emissions.

Benefits

  • Reduced travel time and congestion.
  • Lower fuel consumption and emissions.
  • Improved emergency response through priority signaling.

Upcoming Projects

  • AI-driven traffic lights in major intersections (2025).
  • Smart parking systems integrated with traffic apps (2024).
  • Connected vehicle technology for real-time traffic updates (2026).
