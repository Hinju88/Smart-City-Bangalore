Waste Management

About Waste Management

Waste management involves the collection, transportation, recycling, and disposal of waste to reduce its environmental impact. Technologies like smart bins and AI waste segregation systems enhance efficiency.

Benefits

  • Reduced landfill waste through recycling.
  • Minimized pollution and health hazards.
  • Energy generation from organic waste.

Upcoming Projects

  • City-wide smart bin deployment (2025).
  • Composting hubs for organic waste (2024).
  • Plastic recycling units in urban zones (2026).
