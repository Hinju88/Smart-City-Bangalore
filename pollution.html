Pollution Control

About Pollution Control

Pollution control measures include air quality monitoring, waste treatment, and green initiatives. These efforts are crucial to maintaining public health and sustainability.

Benefits

  • Improved air and water quality.
  • Reduction in diseases caused by pollutants.
  • Preservation of biodiversity and ecosystems.

Upcoming Projects

  • Expansion of air quality monitoring stations (2024).
  • Water purification systems in urban areas (2025).
  • Emission-free public transport rollout (2026).
