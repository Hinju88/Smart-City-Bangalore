Citizen Engagement

About Citizen Engagement

Citizen engagement initiatives aim to involve the public in city planning, feedback mechanisms, and decision-making processes for better governance.

Benefits

  • Stronger community participation in governance.
  • Improved transparency and trust in administration.
  • Efficient solutions driven by citizen feedback.

Upcoming Projects

  • Mobile app for citizen feedback (2024).
  • Interactive workshops on urban planning (2025).
  • Smart city idea contests (2026).
